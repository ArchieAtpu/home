Social Life

What is social life?

Social life can be defined as part of a person’s time spent doing enjoyable things with other individuals. This can either be socially interacted with individuals or something that has to do with social interaction. It consists of the various bonds they form with others, which can either be with family, friends, members of their community or even strangers. It is originally measured using duration and the quality of the social interactions themselves, this can either be in-person or online. The importance of social interaction is that it feeds the need of being driven by a desire for community and belonging. It is also very beneficial due to how it helps people build communication skills and cooperate to achieve a common goal. It occurs between groups of two or more people. They may be more transactional, which is where one person is trying to get the other to behave a certain way, or they may be more cooperative and wants mutual pleasure. A series of studies prove that the number of social interactions matters: Spending more time around other people increases an individual’s happiness and satisfaction with life.

How has the internet played a huge role in social life?

The internet has changed business, education, government, healthcare and the way we interact with each other. It is now the key of social evolution, the changes are of particular significance, it has removed all communication barriers. We use internet in our daily lives. For instance, it helps us receive notifications and emails from others, but apart from this, people can use the internet in different ways like money transfers, shopping order, online food, etc. The internet can serve many beneficial roles in the world moving towards “networked individualism.” Examples of these network individualism are like emails, emails allow people to get help from their social networks, it lets them gather information which then can be used for support and information that can solve important decisions we make. The internet has started to become a bigger place for socializing due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Humans have decided to socialize through the internet which may have negative impacts on their physical and mental well-being. Some of the ways people can engage with others using modern technology are:

  • Using an online video chat platform where you can connect with friends and family. Using online video chats can help host virtual parties or holiday celebrations in favour of the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • Joining online clubs like reading books, running, music, knitting, writing or spiritual clubs. Online clubs can serve many different varieties of hobbies and skills
  • Online classes. If you need help with academic purposes, you can take online classes to help you reach your future goals.
  • You can stay active using online, by joining online workouts, you can keep those endorphins going.

What is social media?

Social media can be defined as interactive technologies that allow people to share and exchange information, ideas, interests and other forms of expressions. Some of the common features of social media:

  1. It is interactive Web 2.0 Internet-based applications
  2. User-generated content, like text posts or comments, digital photos or videos and data generated through all online interactions
  3. Create service specific profiles for the website or app which is designed by the social-media organization.
  4. It helps the development of online social networks by connecting user profiles with other groups.

People accesses social medias on their mobiles that offer services like web-based apps. Engaging in these electronic services can create highly interactive platforms through which individuals, communities and organizations can share content posted by users.

Some of the advantages of social media are:

You can reach a large audience:

Millions of people have access to the social media platform where it’s a great opportunity for businesses to reach large pools of people who may be interested in your idea. According to Pew Research Center, this is the percentages of U.S. adults who use social media sites on devices:

  • YouTube: 73%
  • Facebook: 68%
  • Instagram: 35%
  • Pinterest: 29%
  • Snapchat: 27%
  • LinkedIn: 25%
  • Twitter: 24%

You will have a direct connection with your audience:

Social medias allows you to connect directly with your audience, you would easily find out who interested in your business because on social media like Instagram, they will choose to follow your account.

Organic Content:

Social media gives you the ability to post organic content for free which is a very helpful advantage for businesses. Organic content allows you to post as much content as you’d like to help engage your audience.

Create Viral Content:

The most unique advantage of social media would be the ability to post and create viral content which can help you be more famous on apps and your product will be more visible to more people. Viral content can give a wide reach which audience with different perspectives can take a look at your product.

Some disadvantages of social media are:

Lack of Emotional Connection:

Social media has placed a barrier for the way we socialize with emotions. Social media has separated our emotions and separating one’s feelings. Social media has limited to textual content through social media which lacks on personal feelings and connections.

Present Physically but not Mentally:

Social media is one of the major reasons behind health issues like depression, stress and anxiety. This is because, we, humans are missing real-time with friends instead of interacting with them. Relationships of love and friendship have been left behind and now all relationships are being handled through social media.

Lack of Quality Time with Family and Friends:

Social media has caused relationships to be distant. Individuals use their quality time on their devices instead of socializing and interacting with their family members and discussing issues between each other. Nowadays, family members are s distant from their loved ones and prefer to spend time alone on devices.

Cyberbullying

Many people, more specifically kids are going through tough times and are now victims of cyberbullying. Cyberbullying has been much easier for individuals to create fake profiles and threaten people. This has caused many suicides, depression issues, etc. Bystanders have used these opportunities to spread rumours and fake news which isn’t healthy for our society.

Hacking:

Hacking has become a very popular threat of social media. Hackers have found it easy to hack someone’s account and data which can cause serious issues in a person’s life.
