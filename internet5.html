Pros and Cons (Internet)

Advantages of the Internet:

  • Communication, Socializing, and Connectivity
  • Information, Knowledge, and Learning
  • Address, Mapping and Contact Information
  • Selling and Making Money
  • Banking and Shopping
  • Entertainment

Disadvantages of the Internet:

  • Distraction, Addiction and Time-washer
  • Bullying, Trolls, Stalking and Crime
  • Spam and Advertising
  • Pornographic and violent images
  • Identity theft, hacking, viruses, and cheating

Pros:

Advantages of Communication, Socializing, and Connectivity:

The benefits of communicated and socializing with individuals are unlimited. Socializing serves many purposes such as increasing your physical health. People who tend to be engaged in a relationship with a friend tend to be more active, improving their physical health through their social activities. Individuals also tend to be motivated to maintain their health so they can keep up with their peers. Communication also helps boost your immune system! A series of studies prove that socially engaging can increase your immune system, which allows you to fight off colds, and other aliments, more easily with no obstacles. Socializing can also help to have better-eating habits. Connectivity gives you the ability to feel a more positive outlook on your life. Staying connected, shows us the connection to the world which then increases our sense of belonging. Communication improves our mental sharpness. Communication keeps our brains active and engaged which sharpens the minds and reduces the risk of memory loss. By staying actively social, you tend to live long and happy lives because it benefits from having a support system of peers who understand what they are going through.

Advantages of Information, Knowledge, and Learning:

Advantages of knowledge management include better and faster decision-making. When you have more knowledge and information in your head, you tend to make better decisions and look much more professional. Information gathered can help individuals’ problem-solve much faster. Not only that, learning new things can help in the future when it comes to job interviews or a presentation. With more knowledge on certain topics, individuals will get a sense of understanding which will give them the ability to have better communications with others. In the future, individuals can use their knowledge gained from their previous years to create better and interesting products and services according to their job. It also helps develop better strategies among individuals.

Advantages of Entertainment:

The advantages of entertainment can be it relieves stress. When someone is under stress or anxiety, the best thing to do is to get a worthy distraction. Entertainment is one of the best distractions an individual can get, when you spend a quality amount of time for entertainment, your mind thinks of other things which release the endorphins and hormones that are responsible for feeling good. The industry of entertainment is served as one of the largest employments. This can be ranging from street magicians to the galaxy of stars we see on TV, they all earn a living because of entertainment. This plays a perfect role in fostering the economy. Not only that but human culture is revolved around entertainment. Many people choose to go to the movies while on a date or meet with friends and watch a game together. There are many ways entertainment plays in nurturing and maintaining culture. Entertainment serves talent and creativity. Many individuals live off of entertainment. Entertainment has been established to be one of the top industries that focuses on promoting talent and creativity.

Cons:

Disadvantages of Distraction, Addiction and Time-washer:

Distraction can serve many disadvantages for all ages ranging from youngsters to seniors. When individuals are interrupted while doing a task, many will forget an important step in the process because of the distraction. Many will even forget what they were writing which then wastes time to think over the whole task to gain your memory on the task back. When you switch your focus from one task to another, you compromise your effectiveness at work. Even small distractions can reduce production capacity according to Business News Daily. Addictions and time-washer can become a major problem for youngsters mainly. Youngsters in the present day have started to spend more time on devices either socializing or playing video games, this causes them to be addicted to their entertainment which they tend to not do their work or assignment. Abusing the internet can cause individuals to lose their time at work and tend to stay up late working on their assignments.

Disadvantages of Bullying, Trolls, Stalking and Crime:

Bullying, more specifically cyberbullying has become one of the biggest threats to humanity. Individuals have started to take advantage of the internet and abusing the power of different types of social media. Individuals choose to create fake profiles to threaten individuals believing it’s not possible to get caught. Cyberbullying has been starting to spread to individuals’ day-to-day lives. Bullying has also become a risk of depression and suicide and because cyberbullying doesn’t occur in school, it is out of their hands to do anything about it. The numbers have been increasing of depressive state for younger kids. Many youngsters have decided to commit suicide or have gotten suicidal thoughts. Trolls and stalking can also give individuals thoughts of suicide and fear of being alone. Stalking gives people anxiety and panic attacks which affect their concentration and attending things, that may be mandatory. Furthermore, personalities can change to more suspicious, introverted and aggressive. An individual will start to trust fewer people and become more self-isolated. This can affect their sleep as they may be facing nightmares of the situation that has gotten out of their hands.
